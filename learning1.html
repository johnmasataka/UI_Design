The Big Picture

Learning Objective:
Understand how points stack up into games, games into sets, and sets into matches.
Remember the Tennis Scoring

Point → Game → Set → Match
Points are the smallest unit of scoring. You need 4 points to win a game, but must win by 2.
You win a game by winning enough points. Typically, you need to win 6 games to win a set.
Sets are made of games. You usually need to win 2 sets to win a match (3 in Grand Slams).
The match is won by winning the required number of sets. That’s how you win the whole thing!
