Deuce & Advantage

Learning Objective:
Learn the concepts of deuce and advantage through an interactive tennis game simulation, where score progression and all possible outcomes are dynamically demonstrated.
What is a Deuce?
When both players have 40 points, the game enters deuce.
What is an Advantage?
After a deuce, a player must win 2 consecutive points to win the game.
Remember the win-by-2 points rule!
