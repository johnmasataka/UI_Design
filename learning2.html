Point Progression

Learning Objective:
Understand the order of points and how to win a game.
Remember How Points are Counted
A player must win 4 points to win a game — but the points aren’t counted like 1, 2, 3. Instead, they are:
Love (0) → 15 → 30 → 40 → Game
Love represents 0 points.
This is the first point a player earns.
This is the second point a player earns, and halfway to winning the game.
When players tie 40–40, it is called a deuce.
To win by an adequate margin, a player must lead by 2 points (e.g., 40–30 is a win, but not 15–30).
